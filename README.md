# BALANCE-ML
ML models predicting impaired health-related quality of life in breast cancer patients 

This repository contains the source code for the following paper:

**Development and validation of machine learning models for the time dynamic prognosis of impaired quality of life in non-metastatic breast cancer patients: international data from the BALANCE cohort**

## Authors 
Niclas J Hubel, Thijs G W van der Heijden, Benjamin Murauer, Belle H de Rooij, Kelly M de Ligt, Helena M Verkooijen, Sofie AM Gernaat, Meeke Hoedjes, Volker Arndt, Lonneke V van de Poll-Franse, Bernhard Holzner, Jens Lehmann, on behalf of the EORTC Quality of Life Group
